Common Side Effects (Gentle & Honest)

Some clients experience mild and temporary side effects such as:

  • Nausea
  • Constipation & Diarrhea
  • Mild Fatigue
  • Feeling cold easily

These usually improve as your body adjusts. Hydration + electrolytes help a lot.

Who SHOULD NOT use it?

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have a history of medullary thyroid cancer
  • Have severe gastrointestinal disorders
  • Have uncontrolled pancreatitis
Disclaimer: It is always best to consult with a licensed medical professional before starting tirzepatide.
